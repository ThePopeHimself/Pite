About me

Péter Salamon — Painter & Tattoo Artist Multidisciplinary artist working at the intersection of 3D sculpting, oil painting, and tattoo art. My workflow often begins with digital sculpting (ZBrush), which I translate into large-scale oil paintings, exploring surreal, figurative, and psychological themes. Focused on transformation, inner tension, and the dialogue between organic forms and geometry.
